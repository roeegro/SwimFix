About the System

Initiators of the project are Dr. Raziel Rimer, biomechanics expert and high swimming achiever in his past, and Gera Weiss, Dr. of Computer Science, a swimming enthusiast. Their goal is to establish a swimming style enhancement system. The system receives a video as an input from a swimmer, extracts the locations of his body part, and produces additional data and metrics by which it gives feedback as to the measure of the swimming style. The vision of the system's entrepreneurs is to develop a high-finish system that can be marketed to groups of swimmers and even swimmers' unions. Our goal in the final project was to optimize the ability to extract the various joints through machine learning, and build a user interface that would allow the swimmer to load his video and return as output metrics extracted from the video and save previous results over time.
